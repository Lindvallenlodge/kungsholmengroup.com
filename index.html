About Us

Strollby Stockholm was born from the experience of traveling parents who understand how challenging it can be to move around with young children. Our mission is to make exploring Stockholm simpler and lighter by providing premium baby gear rentals — from high-quality strollers and car seats to travel cots, highchairs, books and toys — all meeting top safety and comfort standards. Whether you’re here for a weekend or a longer stay, we’ve got what you need to keep your little one safe, comfy, and happy while you enjoy the city. Located just a 5-minute walk from the central station T‑Centralen, we make pick-up easy — and we’re always happy to share local tips and family-friendly recommendations to help you make the most of your visit.

FAQ – Renting Baby Gear in Stockholm

Do you deliver strollers to hotels or Airbnb?
Yes! We offer both self-pickup from our location near T‑Centralen and delivery to hotels, Airbnb stays, private homes, Stockholm’s airports, and even to areas outside central Stockholm.

What age range is your gear suitable for?
Our strollers, car seats, and other baby gear are suitable from birth up to around 5 years old. We have different options depending on your child’s size and your travel plans.

Do you rent travel cots?
Yes – we rent high-quality BabyBjörn travel cots. They’re lightweight, easy to set up, and include a clean fitted sheet for a safe and comfortable sleep.

What car seats do you offer?
We offer both rear-facing and forward-facing car seats for babies and toddlers. All seats meet EU safety standards and are cleaned and checked before each rental. Just let us know your child’s age and weight — we’ll recommend the best fit.

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, especially during busy periods. Last-minute bookings are often possible too — just reach out and we’ll do our best to help.

What if something breaks or gets lost?
Just let us know. We understand that things happen, and we’ll always work with you to find a fair, helpful solution.
