About Us

We are a traveling family living in Stockholm — and we know what it’s like to explore a new city with kids. That’s why we created Strollby Stockholm — to make family travel lighter, easier, and more fun.

Our values are simple: Clean and modern equipment. Reliable delivery and pickup. Personal care.

Because traveling families deserve peace of mind.

To reach as many families as possible, Strollby Stockholm collaborates with trusted platforms like Babonbo, where new customers can easily discover our service, explore our gear, and read verified reviews from fellow parents.
