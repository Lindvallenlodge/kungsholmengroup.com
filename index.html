About Us

Strollby Stockholm was born from the experience of traveling parents who understand the challenges of moving around with young children. We provide a premium rental service for baby and toddler gear, offering high-quality strollers, car seats, and travel cots that meet the highest safety and comfort standards. Our mission is to make your stay in Stockholm easier and more enjoyable by providing everything you need for your little ones — without the hassle of bringing your own gear.
